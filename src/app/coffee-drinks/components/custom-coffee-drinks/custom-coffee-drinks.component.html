:: CUSTOM-MADE COFFEE DRINKS INSTRUCTIONS ::
 Using the following drop-down menus, you will have the chance to select the elements of your custom-made coffee drink. Each drink is composed of the following elements:

** Coffee Variety **
A selection from more than 60 coffee beans types.

** Coffee Ingredients **
A selection of specific coffee ingredients. Yes, (espresso) coffee can be an ingredient to another coffee.

** Coffee Filter **
There are many filtration processes available in the brewing of coffees, each of which has a different effect on the final taste.

** Coffee Brewing Method **
As with filters, there are several coffee brewing methods available that have an independent effect on the eventual coffee flavour.

** Name and Description **
Finally, after selecting the elements of your coffee, you have to give your new coffee drink name and a short description.










:: YOUR CUSTOM COFFEE DRINK ::





Coffee Drink Name is required and needs to be at least 8 characters long.

Description is required and needs to be at least 50 characters long.



Pressing [ SAVE CHANGES ] submits an http post request to the backend for processing.
